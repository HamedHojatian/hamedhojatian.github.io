Intro

What I am all about.

By and large, I am personally interested in computer and electrical sciences ever since I was kids; Therefore, I studied Electronic Engineering in BSc and it was the best experience for me because it opened my mind about electronic circuits and its challenges which are related to HW limitation. For the MSc I decided to study telecommunication engineering because I saw many interesting fields related to it. Then I chose to study and research in wireless networks, specifically the next generation of wireless networks (5G). In parallel I joined the largest telecommunication company in my country as RF planner and optimizer engineer. After a while, machine learning techniques draw my attention, and I decided to follow my academic background as PhD student on applied Deep learning techniques in Telecommunication systems in the main land of AI, University of Montréal, QC, Canada , and now here I am.

Isfahan University of Technology (IUT)

Supervisor: Mohammad Javad Omidi

Master of Electrical and Computer Engineering

Isfahan, Iran

My master thesis in general was about time and frequency synchronization in uplink Massive MIMO multiuser system in presence of frequecny error. Considering carrier frequency offset (CFO) and ICI in OFDM system, I developed a method to jointly estimate the channel and CFO. I extended my research to design low complexity receiver for CFO compensation and symbol detection. The result of my thesis has been published in IEEE conference.
